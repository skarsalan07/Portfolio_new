Aimers

AI-powered virtual interview partner.

What is Aimers?

Aimers simulates real-time mock interviews using AI and voice. Get instant feedback and a personalized improvement roadmap.
Tech: React, FastAPI, BERT NLP.

Why not deployed live?
  • AI models need high memory & GPU (not on free hosting).
  • First use: 6–10s model load time.
  • Audio analysis is simulated in demo.
  • Future: Move to lighter/serverless AI.
